Ethio stew (doro wot)

Doro wat is an incredibly fragrant, spicy, flavorful, rich slow-cooked Ethiopian chicken stew.

Doro wat is an onion-based spicy chicken stew usually made during occasions like parties, weddings, birthdays, or any day. It is one of the national dishes of Ethiopia, usually served over injera.

Ingredients

  • 4 diced red onions
  • 4 pounds chicken
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp ethiopian kibe (butter)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 6 boiled eggs
  • 1 lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp berbere spice
  • 1 tbsp salt
